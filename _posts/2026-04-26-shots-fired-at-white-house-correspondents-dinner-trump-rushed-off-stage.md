Shots were fired at the White House Correspondents' Dinner in Washington DC last night — and President Donald Trump was rushed off stage by Secret Service.



The suspect — identified as Cole Tomas Allen, a 31-year-old from California — charged a Secret Service checkpoint armed with multiple weapons before shots rang out inside the Washington Hilton.



Trump, First Lady Melania, and all Cabinet members present are safe and uninjured.



One Secret Service agent was shot and hospitalised. No other injuries were reported.

Tomas Allen is now in custody and faces two charges:

— Using a firearm during a crime of violence

— Assault on a federal officer



Trump later addressed the nation at a hastily organised press conference at the White House — praising the Secret Service for their swift response.



This is the third security incident targeting the US President since his 2024 campaign.
